Honest Ember hot sauce is a small town company with big time ambitions. Each sauce is crafted with all natural ingredients that are hand selected for prefection. We pride ourselves on our sauces so we treat every step of the process with extra care to assure these sauces come out bold and unique.

Honest Ember is based in Austin, Texas but is currently in hot sauce stores nation wide and also ships internationally
